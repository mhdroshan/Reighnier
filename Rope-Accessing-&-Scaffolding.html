Our service

Rope accessing & scaffolding services


Rope accessing

Rope accessing is a technique in which the technician access remote and challenging locations and minimizing potential damage to structure and resources. This method is cost saving and efficient compared to others which involve the use of equipment that hinder work flow. This method is preferable in piping, towers, tunnels, overhead cranes, pressure vessels confined spaces etc.


Our industrial rope access service includes:
General works in off shore, marine& onshore fields Wind mill inspection Inspection & testing of structures Structural surveys Building & industrial cleaning works High pressure hydro blasting & grit blasting Cleaning & painting Any work at height or in areas where access is difficult.


Scaffolding services

Scaffolding is a platform to work from, that provide safety to workers and act as a means of access. In locations with no built in access and platform, scaffold frames can be installed. We provide scaffolding systems made of heavy-duty materials to meet the industry’s high safety standards. We offer scaffolding rental or scaffolds lifting services for a variety of industries, including power plants, refineries, petroleum chemicals, and construction.
